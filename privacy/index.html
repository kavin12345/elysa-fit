Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Elysa ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the Elysa mobile application (the "App"). The App is operated by Kevin Seo, an individual ("Operator").

By using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide

  • Account information. When you sign up, we collect your email address and display name. If you sign in with Google, Google provides us with your name and email associated with your Google account.
  • Profile information. During onboarding and ongoing use, you may provide your sex, date of birth, height, weight, training goal, and weekly training frequency.
  • Workout data. Sets you log (exercise, weight, reps, timestamp), the raw text or transcript of what you said when you logged them, and chat messages you send to the in-app assistant ("Elysa").
  • Voice transcripts. If you use a voice input feature (where available), your speech is transcribed to text on your device or by a speech-to-text provider, and the resulting text is processed the same way as any chat message.

Information collected automatically

  • Device and technical information. App version, operating system version, device model, and an anonymous device identifier used to keep you signed in.
  • Usage information. Authentication tokens issued by our backend and timestamps of requests, used to operate and secure the service.

We do not collect precise location, contacts, photos, microphone audio recordings (only the resulting text), or other device data beyond what is listed above.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and maintain your account and authenticate you.
  • Provide the core App functionality: storing your workouts, retrieving past workouts, and generating personalized assistant responses.
  • Send your chat messages and workout context to third-party AI providers (see Section 3) so the assistant can respond.
  • Personalize the assistant's responses to your profile, goals, and history.
  • Diagnose and fix technical problems.
  • Communicate with you about your account or material changes to the service.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your information for advertising and there are no third-party ad networks integrated into the App.

3. How We Share Information

We share information with the following third-party service providers, each of which processes data on our behalf:

ProviderPurposeWhat we share
Supabase (auth + database hosting) Stores your account, profile, workouts, and chat messages. All account, profile, workout, and chat data described above.
Groq (large language model inference) Powers the assistant's responses. The text of each chat message you send, plus relevant context from your recent workouts and profile, at request time.
OpenAI (text embeddings) Generates numerical embeddings used to retrieve relevant past workouts. The text content of your sets and chat messages. OpenAI's API is used in "no-training" mode: per OpenAI's policy, content sent via the API is not used to train their models.
Google Sign-In (optional) If you choose Google sign-in, Google authenticates you. Your Google account credentials are handled by Google directly; we receive only your name, email, and an identity token.
Google Play Billing (when subscriptions are enabled) Processes subscription purchases and renewals. Your purchase token and subscription status. We do not receive your full payment details.

We may also disclose information when required by law, to enforce our Terms of Service, to protect the security of our users, or in connection with a business transfer (e.g., acquisition or merger).

4. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account from within the App:

  • Your account, profile, workouts, and chat history are scheduled for permanent deletion within 30 days.
  • Backups may take up to 90 additional days to fully purge.
  • We may retain limited information longer if required by law (e.g., financial records related to subscription purchases).

5. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate information (most profile fields are editable from within the App; for anything else, contact us).
  • Delete your account and associated data (use the in-app "Delete Account" feature in Settings).
  • Object to or restrict certain processing.
  • Receive a copy of your data in a portable format.
  • Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below.

California residents: The California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) gives you the rights above and the right not to be discriminated against for exercising them. We do not sell personal information.

EU/UK residents: The legal bases for our processing are (a) performance of a contract with you (operating the App), (b) your consent (where applicable), and (c) our legitimate interests (improving and securing the service). You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

6. Children

The App is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us.

7. Security

We use industry-standard security measures, including encryption in transit (TLS) and encryption at rest provided by our database host. No system is perfectly secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security, but we work to protect your information using reasonable safeguards.

8. International Transfers

Our service providers may process your information in countries other than your country of residence, including the United States. By using the App, you consent to such transfers.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you through the App or by email before the changes take effect. The "Effective date" at the top of this policy reflects the most recent revision.
